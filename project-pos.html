OVERVIEW

Project Overview

A desktop-based business management system designed for LW Mini Mart to replace manual, paper-based operations with a structured and efficient digital solution. The system integrates point-of-sale transactions, inventory management, and financial tracking into a single offline application, making it ideal for small businesses with limited or unreliable internet access.

It enables staff to process sales quickly through an intuitive POS interface while automatically updating inventory levels in real time. Store owners can monitor stock availability, track product expiry, and manage cash flow through detailed reports and analytics.
