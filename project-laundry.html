OVERVIEW

Project Overview

A full-stack web and mobile-based system developed to modernize laundry shop operations in Barangay Mambaling, Cebu City, with a focused implementation for Triple K Laundry Shop. The system addresses the inefficiencies of traditional manual transactions by digitizing the entire workflow—from booking and communication to order tracking and delivery management.

Customers can schedule pickups, monitor the real-time status of their laundry, and receive updates without needing to physically visit the shop. At the same time, administrators gain access to a centralized dashboard to manage users, orders, and riders efficiently, while ensuring accurate tracking of transactions and services.

The platform integrates mobile and web technologies with a cloud-based backend, enabling seamless data synchronization across all users. By combining real-time tracking, role-based access, and delivery coordination, the system improves operational efficiency, enhances customer experience, and introduces a scalable solution for small laundry businesses transitioning into digital services.
